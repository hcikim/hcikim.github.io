  • Introduction

    Taewook (Tommy) Kim is a M.Phil student in the HCI Initiative at HKUST, advised by Prof. Xiaojuan Ma. He earned his B.S. from Hanyang University on February 2018.

    His research in Human-Computer Interaction focuses on Interpersonal Communication and Affective Computing. He is interested in designing interactive systems that facilitate users representing their minds. He builds systems encouraging people more expressive and transparent for their own emotions in Human-Human Communication, to abate stresses and tensions in their everyday lives.
