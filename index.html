About

Taewook Kim is a prospective PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) from Fall 2018. He will be advised by Prof. Xiaojuan Ma as a member of the HCI Initiative.

His research in Human-Computer Interaction focuses on Computational Linguistics and Affective Computing. He is interested in building interactive systems that alleviate users stress and tension in their everyday lives.

He received his B.S. in Business Administration and B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Hanyang University on February 2018.
